Avoid signups from used addresses

The user’s transaction history across dapps shouldn’t be public.

Direct attention away from pending transactions

Human attention span is shorter than the blockchain's update time.

Explain on-chain operations right before requesting them

Wallets don’t clarify what transactions are for and don’t speak the user’s language.

Visually distinguish between on and off-chain data

The user should always know how which data points are public.

Make transaction history accessible

Having an easy way to verify every on-chain interaction with your dapp builds trust.

Bake education into the onboarding flow step-by-step

The user has a model of the Internet that doesn’t always match blockchain reality.

Start with small transactions, ease into larger ones

Users acquire confidence in your dapp gradually, starting big can scare them away.
