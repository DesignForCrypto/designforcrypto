About our project

What

Design For Crypto is a community effort. A list of best practices for user-facing decentralized applications, developed and curated by the open UX community.

Why

Because we believe that in order for blockchain technology to reach its full potential we need to build trust with it and break the barrier that's keeping non-technical savvy users out.

Who

Cande, Ale and the rest of the community
